Roman Numeral Converter

Lets learn how it works:

1. Remember these seven symbols:

1 5 10 50 100 500 1000
I V X L C D M

2. Learn one digit numbers on the right side of the screen:
"I" on the left means...one less then..., "I" on the right means...one more then... and on the right you can use it once, twice or three times.

3. Rules that work for I, V, X symbols, also work for X, L, C and C, D, M.

EXAMPLES:
3888 = 3000 + 800 + 80 + 8 = MMM + DCCC + LXXX + VIII = MMMDCCCLXXXVIII
2457 = 2000 + 400 + 50 + 7 = MM + CD + L + VII = MMCDLVII
